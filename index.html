About us

At Easy Training, we believe technology and sports complement each other, and our goal is to make them accessible to everyone.

We are obsessively passionate about making people's lives easier thru the use of technology.

Our team is composed of a group of individuals with deep expertise in technology and sports, with several years of experience into different cultures and practices.

How it works

Let's show you how Easy Training App works in three simple steps:

  1. The administrator registers the users that will have access to the platform: Instructors/personal trainers and gym students;
  2. The instructor/personal trainer creates one or more training plans for the students he or she are responsible for by relying on a database of pre-defined workouts;
  3. The gym students visualise the training plans assigned by the instructor!
